About Us

Air’N’Craft is an evolutionary platform offering activities, workshops and contests for the creative and geeky minds in crafts and aero sciences. The offerings are designed and planned by field and subject experts to enrich the participants’ knowledge and trigger their creative thinking ability.

The workshops are jointly handled by WeThe5Pilots (An Aero-Modelling start-up focusing on teaching the concepts of Aero Sciences to the aspiring students) and Aydiv Crafts (A brand offering customized gifting solutions and regular workshops / events to tap the imaginative spectrum of every individual)
