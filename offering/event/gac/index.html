Global Aeromodelling Championship is a unique opportunity to connect the youth and lay the foundation for quality aero education. About AIC RAISE

AIC RAISE is India's 1st Startup Incubation Centre focusing on the United Nation's Sustainable Development Goals and established under Atal Innovation Mission of NITI Aayog, Government of India.


About ActiveDabba

Active Dabba is our exclusive online store for creative S.T.E.M activity boxes involving themes like Crafts and Aeromodelling. These kits are tested and approved by age-appropriate users across countries. Every activity kit has certain value adds and surprise games based on the age category of the user.


About SDG Schools

SDG School is a UN SDGs education initiative launched by Indian and Syrian volunteers with an aim to build and raise awareness about the UN's Sustainable Development Goals to make the world a better place. We believe that working collaboratively, locally and globally, will help to achieve the Global Goals before 2030. We start from each community's need, then we create a plan of action that includes individuals and groups of this community.
