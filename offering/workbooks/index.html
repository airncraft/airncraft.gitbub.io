Our Exclusive Workbook Series

Our Workbook Series in English are categorised for two age groups – 4 to 8 years and 8 to 15 years. The workbooks would be delivered to your email every fortnight once you register and pay. Our workbooks guarantee a total of 90 minutes (minimum) of active time and assist you in keeping the kids active and tap their creative quotient. All the worksheets are from a team of passionate professionals with proven models in teaching Crafts and Science to hundreds of kids.

  • 100% Virtual
  • Printer not mandatory for most sheets
  • Instant delivery of the workbooks to your email inbox

    • You simply need to Download > Hand-out > Enjoy

  • Download the workbook
  • Print the sheets (if needed)
  • Hand them out to the kids (One by One)
  • Let them enjoy solving the sheets!

    • You can review their progress and set rewards
