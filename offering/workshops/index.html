Individual Sessions

One day workshops and hourly workshops are conducted that would cover one or two product types in the appropriate sessions.

Our corporate workshops are stress-busting sessions providing fun filled time to all the participants.

Holiday Camps

Our holiday camps are generally aligned with the school vacation or festive breaks. The sessions are based on S.T.E.M Learning principles and aim to achieve the following:

  • Improve focus and concentration on trivial details
  • Assist in acquiring hand-eye coordination and improve the motor skills
  • Kindle the design thinking ability
  • Accelerate the creative skill
  • Boost the confidence because there is no “right answer” in art
  • Help in planning and organising
  • Give a sense of accomplishment
