Activities

Art | Colour Bleed

An intuitive technique of colouring by transferring the pigments from one medium to another. A little fun with paper and water to bring out the creative quotient.

Aero Science | Plane-Dynamics

Simply build and fly aircrafts made out of paper. The forces acting on the aircraft are demonstrated here during the flight. Building and Flying paper planes can clearly demonstrate the basics of aircraft structure and aircraft motion.

Workshops

Art | Stone Art

Perfect for beginners in the Art of learning to transform a stone into something amazing. The blend of the shape and the colours with the right technique evolves into a beautiful masterpiece.

Aero Science | Glider

Designing and Fabricating a Glider to understand the basic idea behind the parts of the plane and the flight. Tweaking the parts can make the flight perform interesting acrobatics.The participant get a crystal clear understanding on the principles of flight.
